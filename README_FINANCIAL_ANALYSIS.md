## ğŸ“ Key Concepts Explained

### Return on Equity (ROE)
Measures how much profit a company generates with shareholders' equity. Acme's 25.2% means shareholders earn $0.25 for every $1 invested.

### Current Ratio
Indicates ability to pay short-term obligations. Acme's 3.00 means $3 in current assets for every $1 of current liabilities.

### Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)
Measures how long cash is tied up in operations. Acme's 83.4 days means ~2.8 months from cash outlay to cash collection.

### Debt-to-Equity
Shows financial leverage. Acme's 0.64 means $0.64 of debt for every $1 of equity - conservative structure.

### Interest Coverage
Measures ability to pay interest. Acme's 8.0x means operating income covers interest 8 times over - very safe.
